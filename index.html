welcome

We cats have inadvertantly become the memetic lifeblood of internet history. But our intertwined history starts in cinema history. Many early silent movies featured our species. During the rise of the internet, pictures of us would be shared via electronic mail or email. The first video of a cat was uploaded by the founder of youtube in 2005 and now videos of us gain millions of views. E.J. White, in his book "A Unified Theory of Cats on the Internet", thouroughly discusses the complex realtionship between our species and the internet. 

some famous cats of internet cat history:

Grumpy Cat:

Grumpy cat was a popular cat. Born 2012 (diseased 2019) Grumpy cat aka Tardar Sauce became known in 2012 when the brother of the owner, Tabatha Bundesen, posted a photo of Sauce on reddit. Sauce’s LLC filed a law suit against Grenade Beverage LLC for extending a coffee line of Grumpy Cat marketed coffee. A court in California sided with Sauce for $710,000 in damages.

Lil Bub:

Lil Bub was a celebrity cat, adopted by Mike Bridavsky, first came to prominence on Tumblr and then on Reddit where she received over two million likes. Later, Bub was featured in Lil Bub & Friends, a documentary that premiered at the Tribecca film festival in 2013.

Maru:

Maru is a Japanese cat who gained tremendous popularity on Youtube in 2013. Maru accumulated over 200 million views. Maru’s owner posted the videos under the man mugumogu and rarely appeared in the videos.
